Take three similar statements like 'I like bacon', 'You hate monkeys', 'They love me' and break them up into beginning, middle and end text boxes below split by commas, (e.g. beginning box would be 'I, you, they'). Once you've filled out the boxes, click 'Make random text' to see what you get.
